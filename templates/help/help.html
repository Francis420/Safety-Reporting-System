System Help

Welcome to the Safety Reporting System help page. Here you can find information on how to use the system.

Creating an Account

To create an account, go to the Sign Up page and fill in the required information.

Submitting a Report

To submit a report, navigate to the Report Incident page and fill in the details of the incident.

Tracking Your Reports

You can track the status of your reports by going to the My Reports page.

Receiving Notifications

Notifications about your reports will be displayed on the Notifications page.
